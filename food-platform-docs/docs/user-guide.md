## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Download/Visit the App**
   - Visit `https://societyfood.app` or install the mobile app

2. **Select Your Role**
   - Choose "Buyer" or "Seller"

3. **Enter Your Details**
   - Name
   - Email address
   - Flat number (for verification)
   - Phone number (optional)

4. **Receive OTP**
   - We'll send a 6-digit code to your email
   - Enter it within 10 minutes

5. **Verify Your Flat**
   - We'll check your flat number against our resident list
   - Admin will verify your account within 24 hours

6. **Start Using**
   - Once approved, you can browse or list food

### Logging In

1. Enter your email
2. Wait for OTP (6 digits)
3. Enter the OTP code
4. You're logged in!

**Note:** If you don't receive OTP:
- Check spam folder
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Contact support@societyfood.com

## For Buyers

### 1. Browsing Available Sellers

**On Home Page:**
1. You'll see cards of all active sellers
2. Each card shows:
   - Seller name
   - Average rating (⭐)
   - Number of reviews
   - "View Menu" button

**Filtering & Searching:**
- **By Rating**: Show 4.5+ stars only
- **By Category**: Veg, Non-Veg, Snacks, Desserts
- **Search**: Search seller name

### 2. Viewing Menus

**Click "View Menu"**
1. See all items seller is offering today
2. Each item shows:
   - Name
   - Description
   - Category
   - Price
   - Quantity selector
   - "Add to Cart" button

**Item Categories:**
- 🥬 **Veg**: Vegetarian items
- 🍗 **Non-Veg**: Meat/chicken items
- 🍪 **Snacks**: Chips, samosas, etc.
- 🍰 **Desserts**: Sweets, cakes, etc.

### 3. Placing an Order

**1. Add Items to Cart**
- Select quantity (1+)
- Click "Add to Cart"
- Can add from multiple sellers (each gets separate order)

**2. View Cart**
- Click cart icon (top right)
- Review items and quantities
- See total price

**3. Add Special Requests**
- Add notes like "Less spicy" or "No onions"
- Optional field

**4. Place Order**
- Click "Confirm Order"
- Order sent to seller
- You'll get order confirmation

**Price:**
- Total = Item price × quantity
- Cash on pickup (no delivery charges)

### 4. Tracking Your Order

**Check Status:**
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Select the order
3. See current status:
   - 🔵 **Pending** - Seller reviewing
   - 🟢 **Accepted** - Seller preparing
   - 🟡 **Ready** - Ready for pickup
   - ✅ **Completed** - Picked up

**Real-time Updates:**
- Status updates appear immediately
- Get notification when order is ready

### 5. Rating Sellers

**After Receiving Order:**
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Select completed order
3. Click "Rate Seller"
4. Choose rating (1-5 stars)
5. (Optional) Write review
6. Submit

**What Your Rating Means:**
- ⭐ 1 - Poor quality
- ⭐⭐ 2 - Below average
- ⭐⭐⭐ 3 - Average
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4 - Good
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 5 - Excellent

### 6. Your Profile

**Edit Profile:**
1. Click "Profile" (top right)
2. Update:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Flat number

**View History:**
- See all past orders
- Check total spent
- View order dates

## For Sellers

### 1. Getting Started

**Registration:**
1. Sign up as "Seller"
2. Enter your details:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Flat number
   - Phone
3. Answer: "What do you cook?"
4. Upload photo (optional)
5. Admin will verify within 24 hours

**After Approval:**
- You can start creating menus
- Can accept orders
- Earnings tracked daily

### 2. Creating Your Menu

**Add New Menu Item:**
1. Go to "My Menu"
2. Click "Add Item"
3. Fill in:
   - **Name**: Dish name (e.g., "Masala Dosa")
   - **Category**: Select one (Veg, Non-Veg, Snacks, Desserts)
   - **Price**: Cost in rupees
   - **Description**: Brief description (e.g., "Crispy dosa with spicy sambar")
   - **Photo** (optional)

**Available Today:**
- Toggle "Available" to show/hide the item
- Buyers only see available items

**Example Menu:**
```
Vegetarian:
- Masala Dosa ₹60
- Idli (3) ₹40
- Sambhar Vada ₹50

Non-Veg:
- Chicken Biryani ₹120
- Mutton Curry ₹100

Desserts:
- Gulab Jamun ₹30
```

### 3. Managing Your Menu

**Update Item:**
1. Go to "My Menu"
2. Click item to edit
3. Change price, description, or availability
4. Save changes

**Remove Item:**
1. Click item
2. Click "Delete"
3. Confirm deletion

**Daily Updates:**
- Update menu every morning
- Toggle availability based on stock
- Buyers can only see available items

### 4. Accepting Orders

**See Incoming Orders:**
1. Go to "Orders" dashboard
2. See list of pending orders
3. Each order shows:
   - Buyer name
   - Items ordered
   - Special requests
   - Total price
   - Order time

**Accept/Reject Order:**
1. Review order details
2. Click "Accept" or "Reject"
3. If reject, include reason

**Why Reject?**
- Ran out of stock
- Can't prepare in time
- Special ingredient unavailable

### 5. Managing Orders

**Prepare Order:**
1. After accepting, note preparation time
2. Prepare food with care
3. Pack safely

**Mark as Ready:**
1. Click "Mark Ready"
2. Order shows as "Ready for Pickup"
3. Buyer gets notification
4. Buyer has 2 hours to pickup

**Order Status:**
- 🔵 **Pending** - Awaiting your response
- 🟢 **Accepted** - You accepted, now preparing
- 🟡 **Ready** - Ready for buyer pickup
- ✅ **Completed** - Buyer picked up
- ❌ **Cancelled** - Order cancelled

### 6. Tracking Earnings

**View Earnings:**
1. Go to "Earnings"
2. See:
   - Daily earnings
   - Total orders
   - Completed orders
   - Average rating

**Payment:**
- Buyers pay in cash on pickup
- No online payment needed
- Weekly summary of earnings

### 7. Managing Your Profile

**Seller Profile:**
1. Go to "Profile"
2. Update:
   - Bio (what you cook)
   - Phone number
   - Photo
   - Bank details (for future payments)

**View Ratings:**
- See average rating
- Read buyer reviews
- Count of ratings received

**Tips to Get Good Ratings:**
- ✅ Prepare fresh, quality food
- ✅ Pack hygienically
- ✅ Accept orders you can fulfill
- ✅ Be on time
- ✅ Be friendly with buyers

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem:** OTP not received
**Solution:**
1. Check email spam folder
2. Wait 2-3 minutes
3. Try requesting OTP again
4. Contact support@societyfood.com

**Problem:** Account not approved
**Solution:**
- Admin approves within 24 hours
- Check email for approval notification
- If delayed, contact support

### Order Issues

**Problem:** Can't place order
**Solution:**
- Ensure seller has items available
- Check total price is acceptable
- Try clearing cache and refreshing

**Problem:** Order was rejected
**Solution:**
- Seller may have run out of stock
- Try ordering from different seller
- Message seller for alternative items

### Seller Issues

**Problem:** Can't accept orders
**Solution:**
- Ensure your menu is active
- Check if items are marked available
- Refresh the page

**Problem:** Earnings not showing
**Solution:**
- Only completed orders count
- Cash must be collected
- Check if order was completed

## FAQ

### For Buyers

**Q: Can I order from multiple sellers at once?**
A: No, each order goes to one seller. You can place multiple separate orders.

**Q: How long does preparation take?**
A: Depends on seller. Usually 15-30 minutes. Check order details.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: Yes, until seller accepts. After that, contact seller directly.

**Q: Is payment online or cash?**
A: Cash on pickup. No online payment.

**Q: What if seller rejects my order?**
A: Order is cancelled. Try ordering from another seller.

### For Sellers

**Q: How much commission do I pay?**
A: Phase 1 is free. Commission starts in Phase 2.

**Q: What if I can't fulfill an order?**
A: Reject the order and explain why.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Cash at pickup in Phase 1. Bank transfer in Phase 2.

**Q: Can I set minimum order value?**
A: Not in Phase 1. Minimum is 1 item.

**Q: How do ratings work?**
A: Buyers rate after receiving order (1-5 stars).

### General

**Q: Is my payment information safe?**
A: No online payments in Phase 1. All cash.

**Q: How do I report a problem?**
A: Email support@societyfood.com with details.

**Q: Can I change my role (buyer to seller)?**
A: Contact support. Admin can help you switch.
