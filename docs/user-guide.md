# User Guide

Welcome to Society App! This guide explains how to use the platform as a resident of your community.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the App

1. Open your web browser
2. Visit: `https://society-app.yourdomain.com`
3. You can also install as a mobile app (PWA):
   - **iOS**: Open in Safari → Share → "Add to Home Screen"
   - **Android**: Chrome menu → "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"

### Creating an Account

1. Click **Sign Up** on the homepage
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Verify your email (check inbox and spam folder)
4. Complete your profile:
   - Full name
   - Phone number
   - Flat number / Address
   - Profile picture (optional)
5. Click **Continue** to complete setup

### Logging In

1. Click **Log In**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**
4. (Optional) Check "Remember me" to stay logged in

## Main Features

### 📬 Daily Digests

**What are digests?**
Daily digests are AI-generated summaries of important discussions from your community's Telegram/WhatsApp groups, delivered each morning.

**Viewing Digests**

1. Click **Digests** in the main menu
2. You'll see a list of recent digests
3. Click any digest to read the full summary
4. Read the key points, highlights, and action items

**Features**:
- **Filter by date**: Use the calendar to view past digests
- **Search**: Search for topics within digests
- **Save for later**: Click the bookmark icon
- **Share**: Share digest via email or link

**Email Delivery**:
- Digests are sent to your email at 7:00 AM daily
- Customize frequency: Go to **Settings** → **Email Preferences**
- Options: Daily, Weekly, or Off

### 🛒 Marketplace (Offers & Requests)

The Marketplace connects residents who have items/services to offer with those seeking them.

#### Browsing Offers

1. Click **Marketplace** → **Offers**
2. See listings from other residents
3. **Filter by**:
   - Category (Furniture, Services, Electronics, etc.)
   - Price range
   - Date posted
   - Distance (optional)

4. Click on an offer to view details:
   - Photos
   - Description
   - Price
   - Contact information
   - Seller reviews (if available)

5. Contact the seller:
   - Click **Contact Seller**
   - Send message or click to view phone number
   - Arrange pickup/delivery details

#### Creating an Offer

1. Click **Create Offer** (or green **+** button)
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Category**: Select category (Furniture, Electronics, etc.)
   - **Title**: Catchy name for your item
   - **Description**: Details about condition, age, features
   - **Price**: Set amount (or "Free")
   - **Photos**: Upload 2-5 high-quality images
   - **Contact**: Phone or email (pre-filled from profile)
3. Review and click **Post Offer**
4. Your offer is now visible to all residents
5. Manage your offer:
   - Click **My Offers** to see your listings
   - Edit or delete anytime
   - Mark as "Sold" when done

#### Browsing Requests

1. Click **Marketplace** → **Requests**
2. Browse what other residents are looking for
3. Filter similar to offers
4. Click on a request to see details
5. If you have what they need:
   - Click **I Have This**
   - Enter your details and message
   - They'll contact you

#### Creating a Request

1. Click **Create Request**
2. Fill in:
   - **Category**: What you're looking for
   - **Title**: What item/service?
   - **Description**: Details about what you need
   - **Budget** (optional): How much you can pay
3. Click **Post Request**
4. Residents with matching offers will see your request
5. They can reach out to you directly

**Tips**:
- Be specific and detailed
- Upload clear photos (if offering)
- Respond quickly to inquiries
- Rate transactions to build trust

### 👤 Profile & Settings

#### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. See your information, offers, requests
3. View your rating and reviews
4. Click **Edit Profile** to update information

#### Account Settings

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Options:
   - **General**: Name, email, phone
   - **Security**: Change password, two-factor authentication
   - **Email Preferences**: Digest frequency, notification types
   - **Privacy**: Control visibility, data sharing
   - **Notifications**: Alert preferences

#### Changing Password

1. Settings → **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter current password and new password
4. Click **Update Password**

### 🔔 Notifications

**Types of Notifications**:
- New digest available
- Someone messaged you in Marketplace
- Matching offers/requests
- Community announcements

**Customize**:
1. Settings → **Notifications**
2. Toggle each notification type on/off
3. Choose: Email, Push, or In-app

## Troubleshooting

### Can't log in?

1. Check if email is correct
2. Use **Forgot Password** link
3. Check spam folder for reset email
4. If still stuck, contact support

### Not receiving digests?

1. Check **Email Preferences** settings
2. Ensure digest frequency is not "Off"
3. Verify your email is correct in profile
4. Check spam folder
5. Contact support

### Posting an offer?

1. Ensure all required fields are filled
2. Upload at least one photo
3. Check file sizes (max 5 MB each)
4. Try again or contact support

### Messaging not working?

1. Ensure you're logged in
2. Refresh the page
3. Check internet connection
4. Try a different browser
5. Clear browser cache and cookies

## FAQ

**Q: Is my contact information visible to everyone?**
A: Only to people who express interest in your offers. You control what shows in your public profile.

**Q: How do digests work?**
A: Our AI reads all messages from your community group and creates a summary. You get a quick update without reading 100+ messages!

**Q: Can I post on behalf of someone else?**
A: Only on your own profile. If managing for someone, contact support.

**Q: How long are listings kept?**
A: Offers last 30 days by default (you can extend). Requests last 60 days.

**Q: What if someone doesn't follow through?**
A: Report through the app. Our team investigates and takes action if needed.

**Q: Can I sell commercial products?**
A: Society App is for individuals. Commercial vendors should contact support for special permissions.

**Q: Is there a fee?**
A: No! All services are free for residents.
